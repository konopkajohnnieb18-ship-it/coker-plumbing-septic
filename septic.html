How Your System Works (30-Second Primer)

  • Tank: Solids settle; effluent flows out.
  • Drain field: Distributes clarified water into soil.
  • Maintenance: Pumping every 3–5 years prevents clogs and field damage.

Drain Fields: Fix vs. Replace

Field problems come from root intrusion, soil saturation, broken lines, or long-delayed pumping. Sometimes a targeted repair (lines, distribution box, baffles) restores performance; other times a full replacement is the long-term fix. We explain both paths and handle permitting/inspections in Escambia County.
